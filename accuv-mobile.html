Design System

When embarking on a new product, it is crucial to have a design system in place. However, it's important to note that the design system is not typically 100% complete before starting the UX design process. Instead, the design system evolves and grows alongside the UX creation. Therefore, we will begin by establishing the Design System Foundations for the new product, and gradually add and refine its content as the design progresses.
